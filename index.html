Workshops

Three days of immersive Indian dance workshops led by master teachers — from classical expression to vibrant folk traditions. All workshops welcome dancers of every background.

Friday 1 May 2026
12:00 – 16:00
Technique & Tools Lab: Pushing the Boundaries of Rajasthani Folk
✦ Colleena ShaktiStudio Aakash

A deep-dive into the foundational tools of performance — how the body communicates beyond steps. Explore breath, gaze, intention and the distinct vocabulary of Rajasthani folk as instruments of expression.

Rajasthani Folk / Abhinaya
17:00 – 18:30
Bollywood Folk Fusion
✦ Vaishali SagarStudio Art Deco

Where Bollywood meets folk — a joyful fusion blending the energy of Indian cinema dance with the vibrancy of regional folk traditions. High energy, full heart.

Bollywood / Folk
18:30 – 20:00
Garba Choreography
✦ Shampa GopikrishnaStudio Art Deco

The spirited circular dance of Gujarat, traditionally performed during Navratri. Rhythmic footwork, graceful arm patterns, and the profound joy of dancing together in a circle.

Garba / Gujarati Folk
16:30 – 17:00  ·  Spill the Chai — Community Break
20:00 – 20:15  ·  Costume Workshop: Kalbeliya Styling
20:15 – 21:00
Community Class: Kalbeliya
✦ Swapnil DagliyaStudio Art Deco

Kalbeliya is the serpentine folk dance of the Kalbelia tribe of Rajasthan, recognised by UNESCO as intangible cultural heritage. Fluid, earthy, and deeply alive. Included with your Opening Party ticket — right before the party begins.

Kalbeliya / Rajasthani FolkIncluded with Opening Party
Saturday 2 May 2026
08:45 – 09:00  ·  Rise & Riyaaz — Morning Warm-Up with Swapnil
09:00 – 11:00
Kuthu: The Heartbeat of the Streets
✦ DesihopStudio Art Deco

Tamil street dance — high energy, rhythmic, rooted in Tamil cinema and celebration culture. Fast footwork, expressive hand gestures, and unstoppable groove.

Kuthu / Tamil Folk
11:00 – 12:30
The Martial Grace of Mayurbhanj Chhau
✦ Shampa GopikrishnaStudio Art Deco

A semi-classical dance rooted in martial arts and tribal traditions from Odisha. Powerful leaps, warrior postures, and deeply expressive storytelling — movement as mythology.

Mayurbhanj Chhau
13:30 – 15:00
Giddha: The Pulse of Punjab
✦ ManasiStudio Ananta

The folk dance of Punjabi women — lively, expressive, full of wit. Participants perform boliyan (folk verses) and clap in rhythmic patterns, celebrating everyday life with collective joy.

Giddha / Punjabi Folk
15:00 – 15:20  ·  Spill the Chai — Community Break
15:20 – 16:50
Abhinaya: Training Expression for Dance
✦ Colleena ShaktiStudio Ananta

The ancient art of expressive storytelling — communicating emotion through facial expression, gesture, and the whole body. Learn to inhabit a character and move an audience.

Abhinaya / Expression
Sunday 3 May 2026
09:15 – 09:30  ·  Rise & Riyaaz — Morning Warm-Up with Swapnil
09:30 – 11:00
Indian Fusion Bellydance
✦ Colleena ShaktiStudio Ananta

A fusion workshop blending Indian folk aesthetics with the fluid isolations of bellydance — where two ancient traditions meet to create something entirely new.

Indian Fusion / Bellydance
11:00 – 12:30
Desi-Afro Grooves: From Mumbai to Lagos
✦ DesihopStudio Ananta

A genre-defying workshop exploring where Indian and African dance traditions collide — rhythm, groove, and movement that crosses continents.

Desi / Afrobeats Fusion
13:30 – 15:30
Lavani: The Bold Heart of Maharashtra
✦ Swapnil DagliyaStudio Ananta

Lavani is a powerful folk form from Maharashtra — strong rhythms, dramatic expression, and the kind of energy that fills a room without warning. Bold, proud, and utterly captivating.

Lavani / Maharashtrian Folk
15:30 – 15:50  ·  Spill the Chai — Community Break
15:50 – 17:20
Bamboo Beats: The Rhythms of Cheraw
✦ Vaishali SagarStudio Ananta

Cheraw is the bamboo dance of Mizoram, northeast India — a striking and playful tradition where dancers weave between moving bamboo poles. Precision, rhythm, and pure joy.

Cheraw / Mizoram Folk
17:20 – 18:00  ·  One Last Gupshup — Closing Circle
